Avant-propos

TheRedChannel is a cybercarnet about state-makers, mark-makers, and state marks by Andrew Papenheim. That is: visual identity, branded communications, the people who make them, and the people who use them. It is also more of a general exercise in the art of egg gathering. Andrew's New Year's resolution was to tweet less. Blogs, essentially tweetstorms to which – blissfully – the reader cannot readily reply, are a decent erstaz. Thanks for visiting.
